# LinkedIn Draft 09: Better Gates Beat More Automation

When automation breaks, my first instinct used to be more automation.

Another model call.

Another retry.

Another parser that accepts stranger input.

Another fallback with a nicer name.

Now I reach for gates earlier.

A gate is the system saying:

this state is unsafe to continue from.

Very boring sentence.

Very useful sentence.

In AI workflows, I like gates around authority and evidence.

Missing source? Block.

Reviewed value conflicts with the system of record? Block.

Export needs a confirmed snapshot? Block until it exists.

The product feels less magical this way.

I can live with that.

The work that is safe should move fast.

The work that needs judgment should surface before it becomes damage.
